MESA - Maximum Entropy Spectral Analysis

Learn MESA formula, spectral analysis, cycle detection, trend/cycle separation, and get code in Python, Rust, Go, and 19 more languages.

Simulator not available for this indicator

Quick Answer

MESA is a spectral analysis tool for traders. Think of it as a microscope for price cycles. It finds the dominant market cycle length and tells you when a cycle is turning. This helps predict trend changes and time entries with cycle turning points.

Formula

Maximum Entropy Spectral Analysis:
1. Autocorrelation analysis of price data
2. Maximum entropy principle applied to spectrum
3. Peak frequency identifies dominant cycle
4. Cycle length = 1 / Dominant Frequency
5. Predicts cyclical turning points

MESA Signal Interpretation

Cycle turning from down to up

Bullish cycle phase - potential upward move starting.

Cycle turning from up to down

Bearish cycle phase - potential downward move starting.

Dominant cycle identified

Cycle length known - helps predict future turns.

Trend component separated

Trend direction visible - trade in trend direction.

Cycle peak detected

Near top of cycle - potential reversal or pause.

How MESA Works

MESA uses maximum entropy spectral analysis to decompose price data into its frequency components. It finds the dominant cycle in the data by analyzing the spectrum of price movement. The indicator identifies the cycle length and phase, helping traders anticipate turning points. MESA separates trend from cycle, showing both the overall direction and the cyclical pattern.

History

Maximum Entropy Spectral Analysis was pioneered in geophysics and signal processing. John Ehlers applied it to financial markets in his books. The method has been used by quantitative traders and hedge funds for cycle analysis.

Best Market Conditions

MESA works best in markets with clear cyclical patterns. It's less effective in random walk markets. Use with trend indicators for best results.

Common Mistakes

  • -Using MESA alone without other indicators
  • -Overcomplicating the analysis
  • -Expecting cycles to repeat perfectly
  • -Using on too short timeframes
  • -Ignoring trend in favor of cycle

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best MESA setting for crypto?

For crypto, MESA works on daily charts with default cycle limit of 40. For Bitcoin, standard settings apply. Altcoins may need adjustment based on volatility.

How to use MESA with MACD?

Use MESA for cycle timing and MACD for momentum. When MESA cycle turns and MACD confirms, signals are much stronger.

What is the dominant cycle in MESA?

The dominant cycle is the strongest frequency component in price data. It represents the most significant cyclical pattern and helps predict turning points.
