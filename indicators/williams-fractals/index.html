Williams Fractals - Reversal: Formula & Strategy

Learn Williams Fractals formula, reversal identification, support/resistance levels, trend confirmation, and get code in Python, Rust, Go, and 19 more languages.

Simulator not available for this indicator

Quick Answer

Williams Fractals identify turning points. Think of them as natural support/resistance markers. Up fractal = high with two lower highs on each side (potential sell). Down fractal = low with two higher lows on each side (potential buy). Simple but powerful.

Formula

Up Fractal = High[2] < High[1] < High[0] > High[-1] > High[-2]
Down Fractal = Low[2] > Low[1] > Low[0] < Low[-1] < Low[-2]
Where:
[0] = Center bar
[1],[2] = Left side bars
[-1],[-2] = Right side bars

How Williams Fractals Work

Williams Fractals identify five-bar patterns where the middle bar is the highest (up fractal) or lowest (down fractal). These patterns represent natural turning points in price. Up fractals become resistance levels. Down fractals become support levels. Fractals work with the Alligator to confirm trend direction.

Step-by-Step Williams Fractals Calculation

1

Look at 5-bar window (2 left, center, 2 right)

2

Up fractal: center high > both left and right highs

3

Down fractal: center low < both left and right lows

4

Mark fractals on chart as arrows

5

Up fractals = resistance, down fractals = support

6

Use with Alligator for trend confirmation

Williams Fractals Trading Strategies

Fractal Reversal Strategy

Buy at down fractal in uptrend. Sell at up fractal in downtrend. Trend-following entries at support/resistance.

beginner

Fractal Breakout Strategy

Enter when price breaks above up fractal (resistance break). Enter short when price breaks below down fractal (support break).

Pros & Cons

Advantages

  • -Identifies natural support/resistance
  • -Works across all markets
  • -Simple pattern recognition
  • -Part of Williams trading system
  • -Good for entry and exit levels

Limitations

  • -Lagging indicator - identifies after pattern forms
  • -Can give many signals in choppy markets
  • -Less effective without trend confirmation
  • -Can repaint on some platforms
  • -Best used with Alligator

History

Williams Fractals were developed by Bill Williams as part of his trading system. Fractals identify natural turning points in price based on fractal geometry. They work with the Alligator to provide a complete trading system.

Best Market Conditions

Fractals work in all market conditions but are most useful with trend confirmation. Use Alligator to identify trend direction. Trade fractals that align with the trend.

Common Mistakes

  • -Using fractals alone without trend confirmation
  • -Trading every fractal signal
  • -Ignoring Alligator for direction
  • -Using fractals in sideways markets
  • -Expecting every fractal to be a reversal

Frequently Asked Questions

How to use Williams Fractals in crypto?

Fractals work well in crypto markets. Use them with Alligator for trend confirmation. Down fractals in uptrend = buy. Up fractals in downtrend = sell.

What is the difference between Fractals and Pivot Points?

Fractals use a 5-bar pattern (2 left, center, 2 right). Pivot points use mathematical calculations. Fractals are pattern-based, pivots are formula-based.

Do Williams Fractals repaint?

Fractals can repaint until the pattern is confirmed with the right side bars. Always wait for the pattern to complete before using as a signal.
