About This Tool

Cloud Storage Cost Estimator for Startups

Why I Built This

As someone who constantly thinks about the financial aspects of running digital services, I noticed that many founders struggle to estimate one of their biggest potential costs: cloud storage expenses.

When you're starting a new project or company, it's hard to predict how much you'll need to budget for storage costs. Will it be $100/month? $1,000? $10,000? This uncertainty can make financial planning difficult.

I created this calculator to give entrepreneurs, developers, and product managers a quick way to estimate their cloud storage costs based on their specific needs. It's not perfect, but it provides a solid starting point for budgeting.

How It Helps Startups

This tool is particularly useful for:

  • Early-stage founders creating financial projections
  • Product managers planning infrastructure budgets
  • Developers comparing cloud provider costs
  • Investors evaluating startup operating costs

The calculator considers different types of data (audio, video, documents, etc.), retention periods, and access patterns to give you a realistic monthly cost estimate as your data grows over time.
