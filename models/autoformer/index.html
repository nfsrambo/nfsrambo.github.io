Autoformer: Advantages vs. Limitations

Autoformer History

Wu et al. introduced Autoformer in 2021, integrating classical series decomposition into the transformer architecture. The model achieved state-of-the-art performance on multiple time series benchmarks with strong seasonal patterns.

Autoformer Implementation Tips

Use the official Autoformer codebase. The moving average window should be tuned based on the data frequency (25 for daily, 7 for weekly). Auto-detect seasonal periods when possible for best performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of time series benefit most from Autoformer?

Autoformer performs best on time series with clear seasonal patterns, such as retail sales, electricity consumption, weather data, and financial series with annual, quarterly, or monthly cycles. It's less beneficial for purely random or non-periodic series.

How does Autoformer handle multiple seasonal periods?

Autoformer can capture multiple seasonal periods through its auto-correlation mechanism, which identifies periodic patterns in the data regardless of the specific period. The model doesn't require manual specification of seasonal periods, though providing hints can improve performance.
